These are the following analysis from the data:

1. Paid apps have better ratings.
2. Apps supporting languages between 10 to 30 have higher ratings.
3. Finanace and book apps have low ratings
4. Apps with a longer description have better ratings
5. On an average, the apps have a rating of 3.5. New apps should aim for an average rating above 3.5.
6. Games and entertainment genre have higher competition. So, entering this genre could be difficult.
